Frequently Asked Questions

How safe is Paragliding and helicopter tours in Nepal?

Paragliding in Nepal is safer since internationally certified pilots are only allowed to fly in the sky.Pilots are much more experienced, and they take care of your safety and comfort. you can be carefree and enjoy the majestic views.

Is there any age limits for praragliding?

Almost there is no such age bar, but it is advisable to avoid small babies ,medically restricated patients and elderly people.

What are the mountain flights and Heli Tour packages?

The mountain flights that are available are Annapurna Base Camp, Everest Base Camp,Macchapuchre Base Camp and heli tour package includes Gosaikunda,Kalinchowk,Muktinath,Kapuche Lake, Rara Lake and as per the customers request.

Which is the best time for helicopter tour in Nepal?

Helicpoter flights are operated on clear days.Visibility and weather should be good to fly. March, April, May and October, November,December are the best months for tour in the Himalayas.

What are the mode of payment accepted?

We accept diffrent kinds of payment method i.e. cash, bank deposit, cheque, fone pay, or any other suitable method within the arrangements made by company. Payments must be done before the flights are taken.

What are the operating hours?

We are open 7 days a week from 10 am to 6 pm.For more details you can call us in our number provied in the website.
